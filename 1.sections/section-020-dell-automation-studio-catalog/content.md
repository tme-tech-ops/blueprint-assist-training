## How to Use the Catalog

### Discovering Blueprints

1. **By Technology**: Browse the eight technology categories above to find infrastructure-focused blueprints
2. **By Industry**: Explore industry-specific blueprints tailored to your vertical market
3. **By Use Case**: Search the catalog for specific business challenges or deployment scenarios

### Accessing Blueprints

Blueprints from the Dell Automation Studio catalog can be:

- **Downloaded** directly into your DAP orchestrator from the portal
- **Customized** using Blueprint Assist (BPA) to adapt them to your requirements
- **Deployed** immediately or after modification
- **Shared** across your organization with visibility controls

### Adapting Catalog Blueprints

While catalog blueprints are production-ready, you often need to adapt them:

```bash
# Download a blueprint from the catalog
# (via the DAP orchestrator portal)

# Customize it with BPA
dap-bpa blueprint lint --file blueprint.yaml --verify
dap-bpa blueprint validate-all --file blueprint.yaml

# Deploy with your customizations
dap-bpa orchestrator blueprints deploy --file blueprint.yaml
```

See **Section 7: Building Blueprints** for the full customization workflow.
